# Building Manager User Guide

## Overview
The Building Manager role is designed for property managers responsible for day-to-day operations of residential or commercial buildings. This guide shows you how to use the DingDong BMS Building Manager Dashboard.

**Test Account:** `admin_manager` / `asade123`

## Dashboard Overview

The Building Manager Dashboard displays:
- **Total Properties:** Number of properties you manage
- **Total Tenants:** Active occupants across your properties
- **Occupancy Rate:** Percentage of occupied units
- **Active Maintenance Requests:** Number of pending/in-progress repairs

### Color Scheme
The Building Manager dashboard uses a **blue theme** with intuitive navigation.

## Common Tasks

### View Maintenance Requests
1. Login with your Building Manager account
2. Click **Maintenance** in the sidebar
3. Filter by status, priority, or property
4. Click on a request to view details
5. Update status and add notes

### Communicate with Tenants
1. Click **Communications** in the sidebar
2. Choose "Send Announcement" for building-wide message
3. Or select a tenant for direct message
4. Type your message and click Send
5. View communication history

### Review Occupancy
1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. Check "Occupancy Rate" metric card
3. Click on "Total Tenants" to see full list
4. Track renewals and move-outs

## Troubleshooting

### Session Timeout
**Problem:** "You have been logged out"  
**Solution:** Log in again. Sessions expire after 15 minutes of inactivity for security.

### Cannot Assign Maintenance
**Problem:** Maintenance staff not showing in list  
**Solution:** Contact your administrator to add staff to your account.

### Tenant Not Appearing
**Problem:** Recently added tenant not in system  
**Solution:** New tenants appear within 24 hours. Contact support if longer.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I reset my password?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive a reset link via email.

**Q: Can I manage multiple properties?**  
A: Yes. Each property is listed separately. Switch between them in the dashboard.

**Q: How long are maintenance records kept?**  
A: Records are archived after 7 years per regulatory requirements.

**Q: Can I send messages outside of business hours?**  
A: Yes, messages can be drafted anytime. Tenants receive notifications based on their preferences.
